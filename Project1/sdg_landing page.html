Clean Water and Sanitation
SDG

Problem statement

Over billions of people do not have acess to safe drinking water and according to a research, around 4.2 billions of people do not have safely managed sanitation services. Everyday, more than 800 children under 5 years of agedie from diarrhea caused by dirty water.


What can be done?

Wastewater treatment, improving water and filter quality can help reduce the situation. Supporting samitation programs, promoting hygiene education and partnerships between goverments also play an important role.
